C / A / S / L

The Clip Art Sound Library is an interactive journey through visual and audio forms of clip art icons.

About the library

The Clip Art Sound Library is a collection of black and white clip art icons shown both in their visual and audio forms, with the audio being a direct translation of the raw data from the visual. The audio form of the icon is activated through hovering.




This library features icons from Flaticon along with paired audio made through raw data conversion using Audacity.
